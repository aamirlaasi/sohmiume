who we are

SOHMIUMe is an advanced materials manufacturer of Multi-Wall Carbon Nanotubes that can be incorporated in resins for various composites.

SOHMIUMe is committed to solving today's challenges by developing new materials to meet the requirements of a changing world and consumer demand for innovative products. The materials of tomorrow need to be stronger, durable, and lightweight to meet the challenges in Energy, Transportation, Aerospace, Life Sciences, and other industries.


our mission

  • 01

    Our focus is to manufactur Carbon Nanotubes, a material that provides the unique breadth of electronic and mechanical properties to develop new applications that were not possible because of material limitations.

  • 02

    We are committed to providing our clients with quality technical support and customer service. We aim to provide our customers prompt, reliable technical advice and information.

  • 03
