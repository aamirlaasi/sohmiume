Our products

SOHMIUMe produces carbon nanotubes that are extraordinarily versatile for composite polymer applications. Carbon Nanotubes are very useful in solving you high temperature and low wear applications. Our carbon nanotubes support high temperature stability combined with durability and strength making it the raw material for extreme applications.

Sohmiume is a technology company focused on manufacturing of multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs) and the development of highly efficient and cost effective process for the production of MWCNT. Sohmiume’s primary goal is to supply the CNT market with a competitively priced MWCNT in order to promote and widen application of the material.

Sohmiume’s vision and belief is for carbon nanotubes to become a part of a new renaissance in material science to improve life. The material must be readily available and accessible by many product developers, which range from DIY, research institutions, small manufacturers to large manufacturers. Sohmiume’s sees the time when innovation after innovation is possible due to carbon nanotubes affordability. The list below shows some examples of applications for MWCNTs.

 

  • -   sporting goods
  • -   wind turbine blades
  • -   military ballistic protection
  • -   liners for industrial tanks
  • -   electronic devices
  • -   lithium-ion batteries
  • -   electrical-shielding
  • -   sensors
