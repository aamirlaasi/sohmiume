about us

NANO-CARBON PROCESSING

The frontier to manufacturing better materials begins at the atom. Technological advances provide us with the capability to see and manipulate nanoparticles that are 10,000 times smaller than the diameter of a human hair.

 

One of these materials is Carbon Nanotubes. As the name suggests, it is composed of a graphene sheet of carbon rolled into a tube structure at the nano level. Carbon nanotubes have unique properties that lend to various applications in medicine, energy, transportation, and specialty materials.

SOHMIUMe is a nano-processing materials company that manufactures Carbon Nanotubes for incorporation into engineering polymers used for structural composite applications where high strength and low weight are required.

 

SOHMIUMe brings materials solutions to our customers, backed by local service coverage. Our advanced proprietary nano-carbon processing technology enables us to further the Carbon Nanotube industry and deliver innovations in polymer integration techniques.

what are CNTs?

Carbon nanotubes are sheets of graphene rolled into a tube. Depending on the type of structure, properties of MWCNTs can range from high strength and low weight to semiconducting or metallic. SOHMIUMe is focused on leveraging the mechanical properties of MWCNTs for applications.

impact of high cost

The high cost of manufacturing CNTs prevents the material from being used in large scale commercial applications. SOHMIUMe's goal is to make MWCNTs available to customers at reduced prices. Image source: By User Mstroeck on en.wikipedia
